Tangible Interfaces Submarine Simulation

The Tangible Interfaces Submarine Simulation project teaches HTML, CSS, and hardware integration through building submarine control instruments.

How to Play:

  • Goal: Find and Navigate to the orange sphere
  • Avoid coral obstacles and stay within boundaries
  • Manage your oxygen and battery levels
  • For students: Edit instruments.html to customize your dashboard. More details at Project Pages
