Pitch Gauge Test

-100 (Down) 0 +100 (Up)

How This Works:

  • The slider updates localStorage.setItem('game_pitch', value)
  • The SVG loads pitch.js internally
  • The pitch.js script polls localStorage every 50ms
  • When it detects a change, it moves the indicator line horizontally
  • Range: -100 (nose down) to +100 (nose up)
